Exploring new investment opportunities

The role of financial experts in world civilization is supportive; their aim is to contribute to the development of industrial production and to provide investments’ security. Actually, it’s the role of a mediator between finance and the real economy.

The contemporary world vastly differs from the world of the recent past. If earlier history was written by rulers, governors, politicians and warlords, now the destiny of the world depends on finance and its experts. However, while the “butterfly effect” described in the fantastic tale of Ray Bradbury that has changed the future is just hyperbole or a fantasy, the “financier effect” is quite real.

Due to the growth of the financial sphere and its influence on political and state authorities, the responsibility of financial experts, obviously, grows. Thus, the modern world imposes the same requirements on them as on sovereign rulers.

Dmitry Bugaenko

Founder and main shareholder of Hadlerco.

ABOUT FOUNDER

Dmitry Bugaenko was born on December 15, 1966, in Ukrainian family: his parents originally come from Chernigov region in northern Ukraine. His mother was a medical nurse; his father graduated from the Kharkov military school.

Dmitry has a solid academic background: in 1984, he graduated with honors from a high school in USSR. In 1992, he got an honors degree in Economic Cybernetics at Lomonosov Moscow State University.

In 1993 – 2014, he guided various business projects in the post-Soviet space, mostly in Russia and Ukraine.

In 2014-2020 – as soon as the relations between Russian Federation and Ukraine began to deteriorate, Dmitriy Bugaenko started the process of closing or transfering his businesses in Russia and Ukraine.

Consequently, Mr. Bugaenko emigrated to Cyprus in 2020 and focused on European business projects including Hadlerco.

Dmitry Bugaenko contributes to socially responsible projects. For instance, he finances the edition of books in finance, supports scholarship programs for talented students of the Faculty of Economics at Lomonosov Moscow State University, runs series of masterclasses in universities, contributes to contemporary art and even collects paintings of artists of the 60’s from Ukraine and Russia.
